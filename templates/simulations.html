The Power of Perspective Taking

Building Understanding

These simulations are designed to provide brief insights into aspects of different mental health experiences to foster greater understanding and empathy.

Reality vs. Simulation

While these simulations offer valuable perspective, they cannot fully represent the complex, ongoing experience of living with a mental health condition.

Supporting Others

Use these insights to better support those experiencing mental health challenges. Sometimes understanding is the first step toward meaningful support.
